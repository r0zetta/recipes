Chinese ribs

Mise en place
Pork ribs, 1kg

Marinade:
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 thumb ginger, finely chopped
8 fresh small red chilis, finely chopped
4 tbsp soft brown sugar
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp soy sauce
4 cl whiskey or sake
3 pieces of star anise, broken into pieces
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
2 tsp fennel seeds
1 tsp cloves
1 tbsp sake
1 tsp szechuan peppercorns (optional)

FiringL
Cut the ribs into pieces along each bone and place in a plastic bag. Pour the marinade into the bag and massage it into the ribs. Close the bag and leave overnight.

Tip the ribs and marinade into a baking dish and mix evenly. Loosely cover the dish with foil and place into an oven at 180C. Bake for 1 hour, occasionally stirring the contents to coat the ribs. Remove the foil, stir to coat the ribs and drizzle some extra honey and soft brown sugar on the ribs and continue to bake for another hour, stirring more frequently until the sauce becomes sticky and coats the ribs.
