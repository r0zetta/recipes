Chicken 65

Mise en place
800g chicken breast cut into strips about 1cm thick

Ginger garlic paste

Curry leaves

Green chilis

Masala Spice rub (see below)

Masala:
1 tbsp Kashmiri chili powder
1/4 tsp garam masala
1 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp coriander powder
1/4 tsp cumin powder
pinch of salt
1/2 tsp corn starch
1 tsp rice flour
1/4 tsp ajinomoto
1/4 tsp citric acid
1/2 tsp curry leaf powder

Firing:
Mix cubes of chicken with ginger-garlic paste and salt and leave overnight in the fridge.

Coat dry chicken pieces with the masala.
Deep fry chicken pieces.
Deep fry some curry leaves and green chilis
Serve.
