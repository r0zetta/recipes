Honey Chicken (Hunaja kana)

Mise en place
4 skinless boneless chicken breasts

2 tbsp butter

3 tbsp honey

3 tbsp crushed mixed peppercorns

1 small packet of baby carrots

Parsley

Frozen curly fries

Firing
In a large frying pan, heat 2 tbsp cooking oil until hot. Lightly salt 4 skinless boneless chicken breasts and place them on the hot oil, searing each side for a few minutes.

Add a few cubes of butter to the pan and baste the chicken. Drizzle about 3 tbsp of honey onto the chicken continue cooking for a minute or two.

Sprinkle 2-3 tbsp of crushed mixed peppercorns (pippuriseos) onto the chicken. Add one small carton of cooking cream and mix. Stir and allow the sauce to simmer and thicken for a few minutes. It should start to slightly stick around the bottom edges of the pan.

Serve with curly fries and a small amount of boiled baby carrots with parsley.
