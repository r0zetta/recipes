Mint Chutney

Mise en place
1 bunch coriander, chopped
2 bunch mint, remove leaves
4 green chilis
1 tbsp cumin seeds
1 clove garlic
1 piece of ginger, about 3 cm, chopped
salt to taste

Preparation
Put all the ingredients into a food processor and create a fine paste. Add a
little water if needed while processing.

Place the paste into a bowl and add 2 tsp sugar and 1-2 tbsp water and mix.
